GEOMETRIC (Blake Velnosky)

This is me.

My name is Blake Velnosky and I've been writing music for over six years now. I started working on GEOMETRIC in 2016 when I wanted to push my music out into the world and grow my sound. I'm from a very small town in East Texas, so I spend a lot of my time writing out my next work in the great indoors. 

ABOUT GEOMETRIC

GEOMETRIC as an idea was born in early 2018 when a beautiful, but rather simple, song called For One Moment was released with emotional vocal chops and accompanying orchestral strings. A month later, GEOMETRIC saw an EP release of four songs with elaborate melodies, beautiful orchestral components, and redemption filled fire. While much grittier and hard-hitting than expected, Illusion began the next phase into a much more vibrant, dream-filled world with its title Memories. The next few months saw remix releases displaying, not only a sharpening of skill and style but what the future had to hold. Winter arrived bringing two single releases displaying the prepossessing, inspiring and mood-lifting style that would accompany the upcoming 16 track album they belong to.

January 15th, 2019 saw GEOMETRIC's debut album, titled 'Beyond Earth', inducing vast, other-worldly illustrations with its heavily orchestral soundtrack blended with soothing electronic pads and soul-stirring vocal melodies. From the ethereal strings on The Hills to the firey brass on Euphoria to the conclusive, hugging choir in Moon Phases, 'Beyond Earth' utilizes the emotionally provocative nature of the symphony and contagious melodies of electronic instrumentation to build worlds inside listener's heads. Since then, the artist has released groundbreaking songs like Bye, which blends sassy, rebelliant pop vocals with modern melodies to create an anthem against worries, and Just Want To which fills the air with unforgiving chants and low-end bass that shakes the ground when listened to. GEOMETRIC is now writing his sophomore album to follow up Beyond Earth that sorts through the sounds of joy and self-exploration.
